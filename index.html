A must-read story

When the oil rush struck Pennsylvania in 1863, one of the prospectors from Philadelphia decided to search for the oil in his hometown instead of traveling to Northwestern Pennsylvania.

He didn't strike oil but he found something way more interesting. He and his family deside to hide the well and keept in in secret for 150 years

And only in 2018, his descendants can finally introduce this delicious drink to the world. And drink made from honey, extracted from the honey well located under Philadelphia.
